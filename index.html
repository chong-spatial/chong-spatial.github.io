Research

My research aims to enable users to better understand machine learning, data mining, and statistical models and results by leveraging interactive data visualizations. Specifically, my research interest lies in:

  • Multi-Dimensional Visual Analytics
    • Explore the advantages of visualization in the process of dimension reduction, clustering, classification, variable selection, and multivariate regression.
  • Geospatial Visual Analytics
    • Develop new geovisualizations to reduce misleading interpretation of results in spatial searching, spatial regression, spatial clustering, etc.
  • Deep Learning in Imagery
